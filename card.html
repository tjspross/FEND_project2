Jigglypuff

When Jigglypuff's big eyes begin to quiver, everyone becomes sleepy as it sings a lullaby.

  • Scientific Name: Jigglypuff
  • Average Height: 1.01 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 17 years
  • Habitat: Mountainous grass

Jigglypuff is a round, pink ball with pointed ears and large, blue eyes. It has rubbery, balloon-like skin and small, stubby arms and somewhat long feet. On top of its head is a curled tuft of fur.
